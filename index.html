WHAT IS GAMEATHON?

Gameathon is an annual event where people get together and make a game over the 24 hours and this year......its going to be bigger!!


The event is organized by the Department of Information Science and Engineering, Jyothy Institute of Technology under BitsNBytes.
It is an annual exhilarating contest for beginners and veterans alike to develop their innovative ideas into an engaging game. This gives an opportunity to every eager Game Developer to learn and master the skills required to create their own masterpiece.

About the college
Jyothy Institute of Technology was established in the year 2011 under the leadership of Karnataka Rajyothsava Awardee, Dharmika Pravara, Late Dr. B.N.V Subrahmanya, visionary par excellence. Since inception, JIT has grown to be one of the reputed technical institutions in the city of Bengaluru. The main focus of our Institution is to empower the students with the kind of skill sets desired by the industry. This would help all students to compete in the highly demanding global industrial market. Our institution is blessed with a pool of highly qualified, talented, committed teaching and support staff to ensure a worthy learning environment for our students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We're here to answer! If you don't see your question here, get in touch with us via our Contact Page

Gameathon is a 24-hour game creation marathon event. It is a platform for aspiring developers to bring together their ideas into a game that they can call their own. They are supposed to create a working game that fits the given theme.

A week of (optional) paid training will be provided to participants on the tools and software used for game development.

Yes, a team of 2 to 4 members is required to participate. If you are alone and you are interested in finding one, we will do a group forming event at the beginning of the event in which everyone will be assigned to a team.

Gameathon provides a lot of other experiences next to making a game. We like to hang out, do fun social activities, welcome mistakes and not be too serious about our presentations... It is games after all :-)

All the participants will be awarded e-certificates. But cash prizes of upto $1000 are up for the grabs, with winners for different categories.
