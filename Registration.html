Guidelines

1. Theme for the Game to be developed will be announced 12 hours before the start of the event.
2. All the participating Teams must submit the Abstract of their game that contains: game idea, inspiration
and Character concept (draft) in PDF format 1 hour before the start of the event.
3. Any changes after the submission of the Abstract cannot be made without notifying the mentors.
4. The game developed during the 24 hour event time should be based on the abstract submitted by the team.
5. If Any part of the game (i.e. character, music) is copied; Warning will be issued.
6. If any part of the game is copied even after the first warning, the team will be disqualified.
7. The characters must be designed by the team from scratch and if necessary, proof of it must be provided.
8. If the Characters are copied/ downloaded; the allotted points for the Character will not be given.
9. The teams must submit their Game project folders along with any playable exe/apk.
10. Online mentor-ship will be provided, a Google-meet will be up and running for the entire duration of the event, where the teams can clear doubts and raise issues.
11. The participants are expected to code the entire game during the event. Hence no precooked codes are allowed.
12. The Event will begin at 11am IST on 15th Nov 2022 and end on 26th Nov at 11am IST.
13. Participants can be asked to share their screens at any time during the event by the mentors to see the work-progress.
